Artist Website

The artist wanted to redesign her website for a more professional and cleaner look. The website also needed an update to include newer projects that she could show to clients. Here's what it looked like before:

And here is what it looks like after:

The artist created her own design and I translated it into HTML, CSS, and some JavaScript. I used JavaScript to create a drop down menu with features such as closing all other tabs when one is opened and highlighting the tab that is being hovered over. I also included a Jquery library that scrolls to to the top of the page when a "top" link in the footer is clicked.
