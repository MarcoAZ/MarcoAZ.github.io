Cat Fight

With custom sprite animations and boss AI, Cat Fight is my most ambitious game so far. Armed with only your claws and some tactical hairballs, you must fight a cat's worst enemies to defend your home.

In addition to the dog, there will be three other bosses: a rat, a vacuum, and a large house plant. Each of them will have unique attacks and require different techniques to defeat them. I plan to make the game playable and testable as each boss level is completed.
