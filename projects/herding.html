Cat Herding!

The very, very first game I ever programmed. Most of it was put together following a tutorial meant to teach students how to make a game with JavaScript. Coupled with my previous knowledge of HTML and CSS, I was able to make some nice adjustments that displayed the score and remaining time in a more unobtrusive way.

As my first game, even seemingly simple things like having a timer count down or moving the cats in several directions was a lengthily trial and error process. However, it was a fun process that led me to continue studying JavaScript for the next year and working on more impressive projects.
